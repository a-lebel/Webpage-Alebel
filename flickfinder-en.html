FlickFinder

Client: Personal project

2024

FlickFinder is a responsive application developed with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Redux, designed to help users discover and search for movies easily and efficiently. Users can search by genre, year or actors and access detailed information about each movie. The application also offers a “Random Pick of the Day” to suggest a movie to those undecided on what to watch.
