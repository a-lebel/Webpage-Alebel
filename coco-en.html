El CoCo app

Client: EL CoCo

2020

El Coco is an innovative mobile application that helps consumers make informed decisions about the food products they buy. By scanning products in real time, users can evaluate their impact on health and the environment, accessing detailed information on nutritional content and ingredients. In addition, the app offers healthy recipes and educational articles to promote a healthy lifestyle.

The design of El Coco is based on extensive research into user needs, resulting in an intuitive and attractive interface that facilitates access to information. A consistent visual style was developed, including typography, color palette and iconography, creating a seamless user experience. Key screens, such as the home screen and scan results, are designed to be user-friendly and understandable, allowing users to easily interact with the application.

The app also includes a customizable user profile, a scanned product history and product comparison tool, as well as features to search for recipes and access short lessons on healthy habits. Available for iOS and Android, El Coco has been well received for its ease of use and appealing aesthetics, helping consumers make healthier and more conscious choices. This project underscores the importance of user-centered design and how a well-executed interface can positively influence consumer behavior.
